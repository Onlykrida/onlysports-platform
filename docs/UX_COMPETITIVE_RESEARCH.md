# OnlyKrida: Competitive Intelligence and UX Research Report

**Date**: March 31, 2026

## Top 5 MVP Priorities (from research)

1. **"Who viewed your profile" for athletes** — #1 engagement driver (LinkedIn's entire premium runs on this)
2. **Role-adaptive home screens** — Athletes see feed + opportunities. Scouts see search + AI recs. Coaches see team dashboard.
3. **Scout search with dense, filterable results** — Data-table layout, batch actions, fit scores. If scouts can't find athletes efficiently, platform has no value.
4. **Opportunity board with AI matching** — Trials, scholarships, team openings matched to athletes automatically.
5. **Beep test + growth zones** — Already built. Market heavily. "Never demotivate" is a genuine differentiator.

## Competitive Gap

No competitor addresses the full athlete-to-scout discovery value chain for Indian grassroots sports. PlayO = recreational, Sportz Village = B2B, HUDL = US-only/expensive, Wyscout = pro-only.

## Key Engagement Mechanics

- Scout profile views → athlete retention hook
- Training streaks (non-punitive)
- Coach endorsements on profiles
- Achievement badges (growth-oriented naming)
- Local leaderboards (opt-in, positively framed)
- Weekly progress summaries

## Role-Specific Interface Principles

- **Athlete**: Portfolio-first, visibility metrics, AI coaching, opportunity inbox
- **Scout**: Dense data tables, batch actions, fit scores, watchlist CRM
- **Coach**: Team roster + dual mode (manage team / discover talent)
- **Team/Academy**: Premium feel, application pipeline CRM, brand page
- **Fan**: Content feed, follow/react, local sports, lightweight

## Indian Market Realities

- 95%+ Android, mid-range devices (4GB RAM)
- Keep APK under 30MB, aggressive image compression
- Offline-first design for Tier 2-3 cities
- UPI dominant for payments
- Sports priority: Cricket > Football > Kabaddi > Badminton > Athletics > Hockey
