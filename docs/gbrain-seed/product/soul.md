OnlyKrida is a sports talent discovery platform for Indian grassroots sports.
The mission: remove geography and nepotism from the equation. A scout in Mumbai
should be able to discover a talented striker from Warangal with the same ease
as someone playing in the Elite Academy in Bengaluru.

The name: "Krida" (क्रीड़ा) is Sanskrit for sport/play. "Only" signals exclusivity
and focus — this is the only platform built specifically for this problem in India.

Founded by Anirudh, a former Indian football player who experienced the problem
firsthand. The platform is credibility-first: built by someone who was on the
wrong side of the broken discovery system.

**The core wedge:** Start with football. India has ~1.3M registered players,
most invisible to scouts outside their district. Expand to kabaddi, volleyball,
athletics once the football graph is established.

**Business model (planned):**

- Free for players (always)
- Freemium for scouts (basic discovery free, advanced filters paid)
- SaaS for academies (talent pipeline management)
- Sponsorship matching (Phase 5)

**The moat:** Data network effects. Every player who joins makes the platform
more valuable for scouts. Every scout who joins makes it more valuable for players.
The ML scoring layer (Phase 5) is only possible because of the data accumulated
in Phases 1-4.

**The Warangal test:** Before shipping any feature — does this make it harder
for a scout to miss a talented kid from Warangal? Ship if yes. Deprioritize if no.
