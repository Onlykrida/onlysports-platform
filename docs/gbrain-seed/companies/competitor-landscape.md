Compiled truth as of April 2026. OnlyKrida's competitive context in Indian sports
talent discovery. Update this page after any new research.

**Direct competitors (talent discovery focus):**

SportVot — video-based sports platform, strong in school circuits. Focuses on
event coverage rather than individual player profiles. Weakness: content-heavy,
not data-driven. No structured scout-to-player connection flow. Strength: large
content library, brand recognition in school sports.

Khelo India app (government) — government-backed talent identification scheme.
Focuses on state-level competitions. Weakness: bureaucratic, no continuous
profiling, no scout access layer. Strength: government credibility, district-level
reach. Not a direct threat — more of a data source opportunity.

Dream Sports / Dream11 adjacent platforms — gaming-first, not talent discovery.
Different audience entirely.

EliteFootball India — niche football academies directory. Static, not a platform.
No player profiles, no scout tools. Low threat.

**Indirect competitors:**

WhatsApp groups — the current "platform" for most grassroots scout communication.
Informal, unstructured, powerful. OnlyKrida must be better than the WhatsApp
group, not just better than SportVot.

YouTube channels — coaches post player highlights. No discovery infrastructure.
The videos exist but scouts can't search by position, age, stats.

**International comps (not direct competitors, but benchmark):**

Hudl — US/Europe focused. Video analysis for teams. Not individual player profiles.
Wyscout — professional scouting tool. Too expensive for Indian grassroots. €499/mo.
InStat — same problem. European professional focus.

**The gap OnlyKrida fills:**

No platform combines: structured player profiles + performance data + video +
scout discovery tools + India-specific verification layer + vernacular support.
The grassroots-to-professional pipeline is completely broken in India and no one
has built the infrastructure layer.
