## 0. Executive frame

OnlyKrida is bootstrapped, MVP-stage, and led by Anirudh out of Hyderabad. The goal of the next 180 days is **not** users-at-any-cost. It is to prove three things, in order:

1. **Athlete supply is cheap and self-perpetuating in two cities** (Hyderabad + Bengaluru). Target: 10,000 athlete profiles by 2026-10-31, ≥60% completed, CAC < ₹40/profile.
2. **Scouts and academies will pay** for structured access to that supply. Target: 3 paying scout/academy contracts by 2026-10-31 at ₹12,000–₹30,000/month.
3. **A repeatable acquisition loop exists** (one of the 5 in §5 has K > 0.6). If none clears 0.4 by month 4, the loop hypothesis is wrong — see §10.

Everything else (UAE, Mumbai, Delhi, Northeast, brand sponsorships) is post-October 2026.

## 1. The launch sequence — month-by-month, May 2026 → October 2026

### Month 1 — May 2026: Closed beta, Hyderabad only

**Theme: 50 athletes, 5 scouts, hand-built.**

- **Week 1 (May 4–10).** Lock the closed beta invite list (see §2 for the Hyderabad 50). Personally send WhatsApp invites from Anirudh's number, signed "former Indian footballer." Voice notes outperform text — record one 45-second voice note explaining the platform; use it as the first touch.
- **Week 2 (May 11–17).** Onboard the first 25 athletes by going to grounds in person (LB Stadium, GMC Balayogi SATS, Gachibowli). Sit with each player while they fill the profile. Goal: ≤8 minutes from "open app" to "first highlight uploaded."
- **Week 3 (May 18–24).** Onboard 5 scouts as design partners (free for life in exchange for weekly 30-min feedback call). Target list: Shaji Prabhakaran's network, AIFF Telangana committee scouts, Hyderabad FC reserve scout, two HCA U-19 selectors.
- **Week 4 (May 25–31).** First **real scout query** delivered end-to-end: a scout searches → contacts an athlete → athlete replies. If this loop doesn't fire 5 times in week 4, the product isn't ready for month 2. Hold.
- **Activation event:** [HCA TG20 League](https://telanganatoday.com/hca-to-launch-tg20-league-on-april-29-to-boost-domestic-cricket-in-telangana) launches April 29 with 8 teams and runs June–July. Get a free OnlyKrida banner + QR code at all 32 matches in exchange for posting match recap reels on the OnlyKrida feed. Cost: zero. Reach: ~25,000 spectators across the league.

### Month 2 — June 2026: Hyderabad open beta + Bengaluru seeding

**Theme: 500 athletes, 15 scouts, first pilot conversation.**

- **Open Hyderabad signup.** Drop the invite gate but keep manual onboarding for athletes flagged as "high-potential" by scouts (signal: scout viewed → favorited).
- **Bengaluru seeding trip.** Anirudh flies to Bengaluru week 2 (June 8–12). Meetings in this order:
  1. [Padukone-Dravid Centre for Sports Excellence](https://centreforsports.in/) — pitch as a "talent-pipeline data layer" for their academy graduates.

  3. [BDFA / KSFA office](https://www.ksfa.in/bdfa) — pitch as a digital roster system for Super Division and A Division clubs.
- **Activation event:** Pre-Subroto Cup zonals run May–August across India ([CBSE Pre-Subroto Cup 2026](https://www.indiangovtscheme.com/cbse-pre-subroto-cup-football-tournament-2026/)). Sponsor the Hyderabad zonal qualifier — provide the official roster app for participating schools, in exchange for branded posts on every team profile. Cost: ₹50,000 in-kind.

### Month 3 — July 2026: First paying contract attempt

**Theme: 2,000 athletes, 30 scouts, 1 signed pilot.**

- Convert at least one Bengaluru academy to a paid pilot (see §8 pricing). The pilot is the first commercial validation; without it, month 4 spend should be cut.
- Launch viral loop #2 (athlete tags teammates) and #5 (fitness zone share). See §5 for instrumentation.
- Begin grassroots Kabaddi seeding in Hyderabad (see §2 — Telangana has a strong kabaddi culture; this is a wedge into PKL Season 13 trials).

### Month 4 — August 2026: Subroto Cup national + scout-side push

**Theme: 4,000 athletes, paid pilot #2 signed.**

- [64th Subroto Cup](https://www.mykhel.com/football/64th-subroto-cup-to-kick-off-on-august-19-with-grand-opening-ceremony-377527.html) historically begins late August in Delhi. The 65th edition (2026) is expected late August / early September. Attach as the "official athlete profile partner" for 2 schools per city in our launch markets — they get a digital roster, OnlyKrida gets first-mover branding inside the school football ecosystem.
- Launch the **Scout Pilot Pricing Page** (currently: founders-only deals). Three tiers (see §8). At month-end, target 3 pilots running.

### Month 5 — September 2026: Geographic expansion check

**Theme: 6,500 athletes. Decide: expand to Mumbai or stay deep on HYD+BLR?**

- Decision rule: if HYD weekly active scouts > 12 AND BLR weekly active scouts > 8, open Mumbai. Otherwise hold and double down.

### Month 6 — October 2026: Pivot/scale gate

**Theme: 10,000 athletes, 3 paying contracts, decision day.**

- If ≥ 2 of the 3 north-star metrics are met, raise a small angel round (₹2–4Cr) on the back of the data, hire a Bengaluru community lead, and start UAE seeding (§4).
- If not, narrow to the one functioning loop and rebuild for Q1 2027.

## 5. The 5 viral loops that actually work for sports talent platforms

Loops are ranked by expected K-factor in our context. K-factor = (invites sent per active user) × (invite acceptance rate). Target overall K ≥ 0.6 by month 4; loop #2 alone should hit 0.3+.

### Loop 1 — Coach posts shortlist, players invited

**Mechanic:** Coach (paid or free user) builds a "shortlist" of N players for a tournament. Players not yet on OnlyKrida get an SMS/WhatsApp: "Coach [Name] has shortlisted you for [Event]. Confirm your spot — sign up at onlykrida.com/c/[code]."

- **Estimated K:** 0.4–0.7 (high acceptance because shortlists are status-conferring).
- **Instrument:** `coach_shortlist_invites_sent`, `coach_shortlist_invite_signup_rate`, time-to-signup.
- **A/B test:** SMS vs WhatsApp template; "Confirm your spot" vs "Coach [Name] is looking for you" wording.
- **Goal:** > 35% invite-to-signup conversion within 72 hours. Kill if < 20% by 14 days.

### Loop 2 — Athlete uploads test video, tags teammates

**Mechanic:** After a fitness test (Yo-Yo, sprint, vertical jump) the success modal asks: "Tag 2 teammates who tested with you — they get to see your zone." Tagged users receive a signup link that pre-fills the team association.

- **Estimated K:** 0.3–0.5. This is the one that should compound — every team has 11–22 connected players.
- **Instrument:** `tag_invite_send_rate` (% of testers who tag ≥1), `tag_invite_signup_rate`, `time_to_team_completion` (when does a team graph get to 8+ players?).
- **A/B test:** "Tag 2 teammates" vs "Tag your whole team (max 5)" vs "Tag your captain." Test number-of-tags on signup conversion.
- **Goal:** ≥ 25% of testers tag at least one teammate. ≥ 30% of tagged people sign up within 7 days. Kill the loop, redesign, or replace if tag-send-rate < 15% by 14 days post-launch.

### Loop 3 — Scout-initiated "I want to view your full profile" message

**Mechanic:** Scout searches → finds a partial profile (athlete signed up, profile <40% complete) → clicks "Request full profile." Athlete gets push + WhatsApp: "[Scout Name] from [Club] wants to see your full profile. Complete it to unlock."

- **Estimated K:** N/A (this is an activation loop, not viral). But it's the highest-leverage retention/completion lever.
- **Instrument:** `scout_request_sent`, `profile_completion_rate_after_request`, `48hr_completion_rate`.
- **A/B test:** Scout-name visible vs club-name only (privacy concerns from scout side); WhatsApp vs in-app push only.
- **Goal:** ≥ 50% of athletes who receive a scout request bring their profile to 80%+ within 48 hours.

### Loop 4 — Academy posts trial; applicants must apply through platform

**Mechanic:** Academy posts trial (e.g., "BFC Soccer Schools U-14 trials, June 15"). Application is OnlyKrida-only. Applicants invite friends to apply ("apply with a teammate, get matched into the same drill group").

- **Estimated K:** 0.5–0.9 in burst; near-zero between trials.
- **Instrument:** Per-trial — `trial_views`, `applications_started`, `apply-with-friend rate`, `applicant_signup_rate` (cold).
- **A/B test:** "Apply with a teammate" toggle on vs off; cohort matching (same school, same district).
- **Goal:** ≥ 1 trial per month per partnered academy by Month 3. ≥ 60% of applicants complete profile.

### Loop 5 — Achievement / fitness zone share to WhatsApp / Instagram

**Mechanic:** Athlete moves from Building → Rising. Auto-generated share card (image: "I'm in the Rising zone — Yo-Yo IR1: level 18.4 — onlykrida.com/[handle]"). One-tap share to WhatsApp status, Instagram story, X.

- **Estimated K:** 0.1–0.3. Lower than tag-teammate but compounding (every zone-up triggers another share). The Strava model.
- **Instrument:** `share_card_generated`, `share_button_tapped`, `referral_signups_per_share`. Use UTM-tagged links.
- **A/B test:** Card design (3 visual variants); copy ("I just hit Rising" vs "Beat me at this — I just hit Rising").
- **Goal:** ≥ 40% of zone-ups produce a share. ≥ 0.05 signups per share (i.e., 1 share in 20 brings someone in). Kill the design and iterate if share-rate < 20% after 100 zone-ups observed.

### Cross-cutting instrumentation

Set up an **`invite_events`** table (sender_id, recipient_phone_or_id, channel, loop_id, sent_at, accepted_at, signup_at). Without this single table you cannot compute K-factor cleanly. Add it before launching loop 1.

## 8. Conversion — scout-side monetization

### India price anchor

[Wyscout's lowest tier is ~€299/year (Copper) and Mercury is €399/year](https://360scouting.com/wyscout-alternatives/), with enterprise plans €3,000–€10,000/year. That's roughly ₹27k–₹9L/year. India scouts and academies will not pay European prices. Anchor at **roughly 1/4 of Wyscout** for equivalent functionality, with an India-specific data advantage.

### OnlyKrida pricing tiers (launch in month 3)

| Tier                      | Audience                               | Price (₹)                  | What's included                                                                                    | SLA                         |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | -------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Scout Starter**         | Solo scouts / freelance                | ₹999/month or ₹9,999/year  | Search, 50 contacts/month, basic filters                                                           | Email support, 48hr         |

| **Federation/Enterprise** | AIFF, BCCI state assoc, KSFA, HCA, TFA | Custom (₹5L–₹15L/year)     | White-label, API access, multi-user, structured datasets                                           | SLA contract, dedicated rep |

> _Free 30-day pilot, full feature set. Conversion only if at least one of: (a) scout makes ≥1 verified contact, (b) academy posts ≥1 trial with ≥10 applications, (c) club's roster/digital ops time saved by ≥3 hours/week. Money-back if none hit._

## 10. Failure modes and kill criteria

Founders need pre-committed bailout triggers. Anirudh agrees to these _before_ the next 6 months, not retroactively.

### Month 3 (end of July 2026) — narrow gate

| Metric                         | Threshold | If missed                                               |
| ------------------------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Athletes signed up             | ≥ 1,500   | Don't expand to BLR proper; double down on HYD only     |
| Hyderabad weekly active scouts | ≥ 8       | Pause scout-acquisition spend; rethink scout value prop |
| Best-loop K-factor             | ≥ 0.25    | Kill 4 of 5 loops; rebuild the survivor                 |
| At least 1 paid pilot started  | yes       | Halve burn; founder-only mode for 60 days               |

### Month 6 (end of October 2026) — pivot/scale gate

| Metric                      | Threshold  | If missed → pivot                                                                      |
| --------------------------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Athletes                    | ≥ 8,000    | Wedge is too narrow; consider single-sport vertical (cricket-only, given HCA traction) |
| Paid contracts              | ≥ 3        | Monetization hypothesis broken; switch to data-licensing or federation-only B2B        |
| Best-loop K-factor          | ≥ 0.6      | Loop hypothesis broken; admit you need paid acquisition and rerun unit economics       |
| Verified Weekly Connections | ≥ 200      | Marketplace isn't matching; quality of supply or scout fit is wrong                    |
| Cash runway after month 6   | ≥ 6 months | Raise or shut down — no in-between                                                     |

### Hard kill criteria

If by **2026-12-31**:

- Fewer than 5,000 active athletes, AND
- Zero paid customers, AND
- No clear single-loop K > 0.4

… then the GTM hypothesis is wrong as currently stated. Either pivot to a narrower vertical (e.g., cricket-only HCA partnership), a different buyer (federation-only enterprise sales), or shut down and return capital.
