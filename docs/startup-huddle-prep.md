## THE PROBLEM WE'RE SOLVING

### 1. Athletes Are Invisible

- India has 1.4 billion people but produces a fraction of the athletes it should
- Talent exists in every gully, every village, every small-town ground
- But if you're not in the right academy, the right city, or don't know the right people — nobody sees you
- There is NO platform where an athlete can build a profile, showcase their skills, and get discovered

### 2. Scouting Is Broken

- Scouting in India (and most of the world outside top-tier leagues) is based on:
  - Word of mouth
  - Personal connections
  - Being in the right place at the right time
- Scouts physically travel to a few locations, see a few players, and miss thousands
- There is no centralized system for scouts to search, filter, and discover athletes by sport, position, location, age, or fitness level

### 3. No Objective Data

- Coaches rely on "eye tests" and gut feelings
- There are no standardized fitness assessments accessible to grassroots athletes
- Highlight reels exist on Instagram/YouTube but are scattered, unstructured, and impossible to search
- Scouts have no way to compare athletes objectively

### 4. Middlemen Extract Value

- Sports agents, academy gatekeepers, and "talent managers" charge fees to connect athletes with opportunities
- This creates a pay-to-play system that excludes athletes from lower-income backgrounds
- The people with the most talent often have the least access

## WHAT ONLYKRIDA DOES

### For Athletes (Free to Join)

- Create a sports profile (sport, position, stats, achievements, location)
- Upload highlight reels and performance videos
- Take fitness assessments (built-in beep test for VO2max/endurance)
- Get discovered by scouts, coaches, and teams through AI-powered matching
- Apply to opportunities (trials, scholarships, sponsorships)
- Direct messaging with scouts/coaches — no middlemen

### For Scouts & Coaches

- AI-powered talent discovery across 50+ sports
- Filter athletes by sport, position, age, location, fitness level
- View objective data: stats, beep test results, endurance zones, improvement trends
- 5-dimension scoring: skill, speed, stamina, position match, endurance
- Direct outreach to athletes
- Save and track athletes of interest

### For Teams & Academies

- Post trials and opportunities
- Build rosters and manage athlete pipelines
- View fitness data across their athletes
- Scout dashboard with AI recommendations
